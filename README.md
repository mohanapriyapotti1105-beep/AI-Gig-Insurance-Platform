# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers

## 🧠 Problem
Gig workers lose income due to external disruptions such as weather, pollution, and traffic conditions. At the same time, fraudsters exploit GPS spoofing to create fake claims and drain the system.

---

## 💡 Solution
We propose an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that:
- Detects real-world disruptions using external data sources like weather and traffic
- Automatically triggers claims and instant payouts
- Prevents fraud using multi-layer AI validation instead of relying only on GPS

---

## 🔐 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy

### 1. Differentiation (Genuine Worker vs Fraudster)
Our system differentiates real workers from attackers using:
- Behavioral analysis (continuous movement, realistic speed patterns)
- Detection of GPS anomalies such as sudden jumps or teleportation
- Device validation (single device binding, emulator/root detection)

---

### 2. The Data (Beyond GPS)
We analyze multiple data points beyond GPS coordinates:
- Weather data (rain, heat, pollution levels)
- Traffic and zone activity data
- Worker activity (orders, delivery time, idle patterns)
- Historical disruption and behavior patterns

This multi-source approach helps detect coordinated fraud rings and not just individual attackers.

---

### 3. UX Balance (Fairness for Honest Workers)
We ensure genuine workers are not unfairly penalized:
- Low-risk claims → Instant approval and payout
- Medium-risk claims → Delayed verification
- High-risk claims → Manual review
- Appeal system allows users to re-validate flagged claims

---

## 💰 Weekly Pricing Model
- Workers pay a small weekly premium
- Pricing dynamically adjusts based on risk level and location
- Aligns with the weekly earning cycle of gig workers

---

## ⚙️ Key Features
- AI-based risk assessment
- Intelligent fraud detection
- Automatic claim triggering
- Instant payout processing
- Transparent and fair claim handling

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Our platform replaces simple GPS-based validation with a multi-layer AI-driven trust system. It ensures secure, fair, and automated income protection for gig workers while effectively preventing fraud and coordinated attacks.
