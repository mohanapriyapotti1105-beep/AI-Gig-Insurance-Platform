# AI-Powered Insurance for Gig Workers

## 🧠 Problem
Gig workers lose income due to external disruptions like weather, pollution, and traffic. Fraudsters exploit GPS spoofing to trigger fake claims and drain the system.

---

## 💡 Solution
We propose an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that:
- Detects real-world disruptions using external data (weather, traffic)
- Automatically triggers claims and payouts
- Prevents fraud using multi-layer AI validation instead of relying only on GPS

---

## 🔐 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy

### 1. Differentiation (Real vs Fake Worker)
We differentiate genuine workers from fraudsters using:
- Behavioral analysis (movement continuity, realistic speed)
- Detection of GPS jumps or teleportation
- Device validation (single device binding, emulator/root detection)

---

### 2. The Data (Beyond GPS)
Our system uses multiple data sources:
- Weather APIs (rain, pollution, heat)
- Traffic and zone activity data
- Worker activity (orders, idle time, delivery patterns)
- Historical patterns of disruptions

This multi-source approach helps detect coordinated fraud rings instead of relying on GPS alone.

---

### 3. UX Balance (Fairness for Honest Workers)
We ensure genuine workers are not affected:
- Low risk → Instant payout
- Medium risk → Delayed verification
- High risk → Manual review
- Appeal system for users to re-validate claims

---

## 💰 Weekly Pricing Model
- Workers pay a small weekly premium
- Premium dynamically adjusts based on risk level and location
- Aligns with gig workers' weekly earning cycle

---

## ⚙️ Key Features
- AI-based risk assessment
- Intelligent fraud detection
- Automatic claim triggering
- Instant payout system
- Fair and transparent claim processing

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Our system replaces simple GPS validation with a multi-layer AI-driven trust system, ensuring secure, fair, and automated income protection for gig workers while preventing large-scale fraud.
